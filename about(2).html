Shree Ram Jaanki Alankar Mandir

Shree Ram Jaanki Alankar Mandir is a family legacy, established in the mid-1900s by Shree Samarjeet Singh, a visionary whose passion for fine jewelry and craftsmanship laid the foundation for what our brand has become today. Over the decades, we have grown while remaining true to our heritage of quality and elegance.
Now led byShree Dharamjeet Singh & Brother's, the second generation of our family, we continue to honor the same values of artistry and attention to detail that Shree Samarjeet Singh inspired. Every piece in our collection reflects a blend of tradition and modern design, handcrafted with care to bring you jewelry that not only enhances your beauty but also carries a legacy of skill and dedication.
Thank you for being a part of our story. We look forward to helping you find jewelry that will become a cherished part of yours.
